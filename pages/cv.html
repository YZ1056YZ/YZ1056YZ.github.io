Summary

Yunuo Zhang is a Computer Science Ph.D. candidate at Vanderbilt University, working under the guidance of Dr. Abhishek Dubey. Their research focuses on decision-making under uncertainty and Partially Observable Markov Decision Processes (POMDPs), with particular interest in using Particles-Based approaches to approximate high-dimensional and multi-modal posterior distributions.

Research Areas

  • Partially Observable Markov Decision Processes (POMDPs)
  • Decision-making under Uncertainty
  • Machine Learning
  • Reinforcement Learning
  • Applications in Robotics and Autonomous Cyber-Physical Systems

Key Projects

  • AIROAS (Annealed Importance Resampling for Observation Adaptation Search)
  • Shrinking POMCP for UAV Search and Rescue
  • Policy-Augmented Monte Carlo Tree Search (PA-MCTS)
  • ADA-MCTS (Adaptive Monte Carlo Tree Search)
  • Domain-Knowledge Guided Monte Carlo Tree Search (DG-MCTS)
